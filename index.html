Typical launches are completed within 7 days for the basic package, with full onboarding support included.

No. The package includes a legal structure option so you can start operating without building a license framework from zero.

What payment systems are supported?

Standard integrations include Visa, Mastercard, Crypto, Pix, and Boleto, with additional providers available by request.

Can I use my own branding?

Yes. You can apply your logo, colour direction, and domain from day one. The platform is fully white-labelled.

What markets are available?

The platform includes 30+ popular markets — Forex, Crypto, Stocks, and Commodities — and can be expanded based on your strategy.

What's included in the Basic plan?

Web Traderoom, CRM, Back Office, Dealing Desk, Billing, Liquidity, Servers, KYC, Options, Antifraud, SMS, and Affiliate Module — everything you need to operate a brokerage.

Are there any monthly fees?

No. The Basic plan has a one-time setup fee of $15,000 with $0 monthly charges. You keep 85% of the profit from your traffic.
