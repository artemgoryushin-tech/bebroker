Frequently
Asked Questions

Need to sanity-check whether a sub-brand model fits your affiliate structure? We can walk you through it.

Contact on Telegram
Who is this reseller offer for?

Brokers, networks, and operators with affiliates who want more ownership than a standard rev-share link can offer.

What does the affiliate actually get?

A dedicated brand wrapper with its own positioning, creative, landing flow, and optionally its own domain.

What stays under my control?

Cashier, KYC, CRM, risk settings, compliance logic, reporting, and overall commercial visibility remain centralized.

Is this the same as a full white-label brokerage?

No. A reseller sub-brand gives the affiliate a differentiated front-end without duplicating the entire brokerage stack.

Can each sub-brand use its own domain and creative?

Yes. That is one of the main benefits: the partner can have a distinct market-facing identity while the back-office stays unified.

Do I need separate payments or risk teams for each brand?

No. The goal is to keep those functions centralized while only the partner-facing layer changes.

How quickly can a reseller setup go live?

Launch timing depends on branding scope and operational requirements, but it is designed to move much faster than building a separate brokerage operation.

Can I run more than one sub-brand at once?

Yes. The model works well for separate GEOs, traffic sources, and high-value affiliates that need a more tailored funnel.

Why is this better than sending affiliates to an outside broker?

Because you keep player data, deposits, reporting visibility, and long-term revenue inside your own brokerage ecosystem.
